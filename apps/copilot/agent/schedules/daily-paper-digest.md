Daily new-paper digest.

For each active Project (ask the researcher or use the most recent), call
`new_papers` to find recent papers matching its Hypotheses that are not yet
attached. For each promising paper, briefly say why it is relevant to which
Hypothesis, and whether it appears to **support or contradict** it. If a strong
contradiction shows up, flag it prominently — the researcher wants to know when
the evidence turns against a claim (or when a tracked paper failed to publish or
got a negative review).

Keep it short: a ranked list of the few most decision-relevant papers, each with
its title, year, link, and one line on why it matters. Ground every claim in a
tool result — do not invent papers or findings.
